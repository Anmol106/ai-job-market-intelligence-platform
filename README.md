# 🚀 AI Job Market Intelligence Platform

An AI-powered platform for analyzing the job market through real-time job postings, skill demand analysis, salary insights, and machine learning-driven career recommendations.

> **Status:** 🚧 Under Active Development

---

# 📖 Overview

The AI Job Market Intelligence Platform is designed to help job seekers, students, and professionals understand the rapidly evolving technology job market.

Instead of functioning as another job portal, this platform aggregates job postings, processes them through data engineering and AI pipelines, and transforms them into actionable insights such as:

- 📈 Hiring trends
- 💰 Salary analysis
- 🛠️ Skill demand
- 🏢 Company hiring patterns
- 🤖 AI-powered career recommendations

The long-term goal is to build an intelligent analytics platform that helps users make informed career decisions.
